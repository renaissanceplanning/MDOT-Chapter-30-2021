Reasonableness of Results

Extent of Study Area

The project study area is based on travel time to the zones in which the project is implemented – any zones within 45 minutes by transit (in the project build scenario) are part of the study area as are any zones within 15 minutes by driving (based on MSTM highway skims).

Travel Time Contours to Project

The service area analysis shows that Project 22-10 brings more of the study area within a one-hour travel time by transit. With the introduction of the proposed bus route, service from Westfield Mall to proposed stops to the both and west becomes possible within the mapped 90-minute travel time analysis window.
